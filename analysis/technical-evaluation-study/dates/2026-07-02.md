## Independent grounding review

The completed independent worksheet was compared with the primary audit and
the embedded immutable review packet.

- Provider existence agreement: 20/20.
- Attribute-faithfulness agreement: 16/20.
- Rationale-support agreement: 17/20.
- Hard-constraint agreement: 17/20.
- Independent fully grounded recommendation turns: 1/10.

The low rationale result is meaningful: the historical responses frequently
claimed location, capacity, event, or style fit without catalog evidence.
However, failure to return a provider for every requested need is classified
under expected-need coverage rather than attribute faithfulness. The reviewed
sample predates the V4 category, location, and cross-category rendering fixes.

The provider sync was inspected in response to the review. It already indexed
description, promotion, service highlights, terms, and event types, but
discarded other titled provider-information sections. The implementation now
preserves those sections as `providerNotes`, adds them to the vector document,
and makes them available to deterministic fit scoring.
